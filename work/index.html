Case Study

Rowan

From zero to full operations in 48 hours.

Otherwind deployed an AI agent as Chief of Staff. Not a chatbot. Not an automation script. A persistent team member that handles operations, communications, research, business development, web presence, scheduling, and monitoring. Around the clock.

The Brief

A solo founder launching an advisory business. No team, no budget for hires. Needs everything a first employee would do, but from day one.

Operations

Email management, scheduling, calendar systems, meeting prep, document drafting, invoicing.

Business Development

Market research, prospect identification, outreach campaigns, CRM, pipeline management.

Web Presence

Brand website, service pages, sales decks, pitch materials, SEO, content.

Communications

Email handling, client correspondence, inquiry response, newsletter monitoring.

Research & Intelligence

Industry analysis, competitive monitoring, due diligence, trend tracking.

Monitoring

24/7 inbox monitoring, calendar alerts, system health checks, proactive notifications.

Persistent Identity

SOUL.md defines who Rowan is. Not a prompt template. A living document that evolves with experience. Voice, boundaries, habits, lessons learned.

Structured Memory

Daily logs capture raw context. MEMORY.md distills long-term learnings. Every decision, preference, and lesson persists across sessions.

Self-Improving

When Rowan makes a mistake, it updates its own operating docs. Rules get stricter. Knowledge compounds. The agent gets better every day.

Always On

Heartbeat system checks email, calendar, and monitoring every 30 minutes. Proactive alerts for urgent items. Background work during quiet hours.
